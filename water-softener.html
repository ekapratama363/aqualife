WHAT IS HARD WATER

Where Can It Be Seen?

Hard water contains high levels of minerals like calcium and magnesium, which can cause scaling, discoloration, and the deterioration of household appliances. It can also lead to dry skin, dull hair, and stained laundry.

Did You Know ?

Water hardness varies across regions in Indonesia. Studies show that up to 63% of households in certain areas use water with hardness levels exceeding safe standards. Hard water can significantly impact the quality of water in your home and increase maintenance costs.

Benefits of Using a Water Softener System

Prevents Scale Buildup

Protects pipes, faucets, and appliances from scale buildup caused by hard water, extending their lifespan.

Softer Skin and Hair

Helps retain moisture in your skin and hair, reducing dryness and irritation caused by hard water.

Improves Detergent Efficiency

Soft water allows detergents to work more effectively, leading to cleaner clothes, dishes, and surfaces with less detergent.

Extends Appliance Lifespan

Prevents mineral buildup in washing machines, water heaters, and dishwashers, reducing maintenance costs and extending their lifespan.

Reduces Energy Consumption

Improves water heater efficiency by preventing scale buildup, which in turn reduces energy consumption and utility bills.

Prevents Stains on Dishes

Eliminates cloudy spots and streaks caused by mineral deposits in hard water, keeping your dishes and glassware sparkling clean.
